GiTuns ~ Lyrics For ITunes on Mac

GiTuns is newly-invented iTunes partner. It makes an easier way to fetch and display the right lyric for the track you are playing in iTunes. With GiTuns, you can also control the music playing in a more convenient way. The lyric also will add to the music file so you can view it in iPod , iPad and iPhone. Different from other apps, GiTuns displays lyrics in a new way: The lyrics are downloaded and added into the Lyric-box, auto-scroll itself to the current line of your track.
